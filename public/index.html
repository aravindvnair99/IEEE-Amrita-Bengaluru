Recent Events

We love what we do, check out some of our latest events

Social Net Bee

IEEE-WIE

Social Net Bee, a unique event organized by IEEE-WIE, focused on the myriad techniques of networking, and gave participants an experience of a fusion of teamwork, idea generation and marketing under time constraints.

Robotic Applications

IEEE-WIE and IEEE-RAS

IEEE-WIE and IEEE-RAS collaborated to bring to students a poster making contest, during IEEE week. The contest required participants to design eye-catching and meaningful posters themed 'Robotic Applications'.

Quiz Competition

IEEE PES

The quiz comprised of two rounds. The first round was a set of 20 questions based on the current affairs and quantitative aptitude. The participants were given a minute to write the solution to each question. This was followed by the second round. It involved 10 slides depicting the images of renowned scientists who contributed towards electrical engineering. The participants were given a minute to identify the scientists.

Hybrid Cars

IEEE PELS

The main focuspoint of Dr. K Deepa throughout the talk was to give a look at the future on hybrid cars so that it will motivate thestudents to go further on this area of research

Vehicular Networks and Intelligent Transport System

IEEE Student Branch and ComSoc

IEEE student branch and the student chapter of IEEE ComSoc joined hands to introduce students to vehicular networks and intelligent transport system. A special speaker session was organized, with experienced industry experts who imparted their knowledge in a simple, engaging manner.
