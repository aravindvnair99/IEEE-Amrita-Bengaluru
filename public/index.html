Social Net Bee

IEEE-WIE

Social Net Bee, a unique event organized by IEEE-WIE, focused on the myriad techniques of networking, and gave participants an experience of a fusion of teamwork, idea generation and marketing under time constraints.

Robotic Applications

IEEE-WIE and IEEE-RAS

IEEE-WIE and IEEE-RAS collaborated to bring to students a poster making contest, during IEEE week. The contest required participants to design eye-catching and meaningful posters themed 'Robotic Applications'.
