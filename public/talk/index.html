About The Conference

AI Of Things is a dynamic conference cum hackathon that aims to demonstrate the combinational strength of two technologies that have formed the basis for today's era of computing - The Internet Of Things (IOT) and Artificial Intelligence (AI).

The future lies in creating an automated world that works on the foundation of sensory data, and what better way to collect, interpret and utilize such data to improve such a network than to interweave machine learning with it?

AI Of Things is an effort to bring together budding developers who envision a better world and share a passion for the spirit of competitive building.
