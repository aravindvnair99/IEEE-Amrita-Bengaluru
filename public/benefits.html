Benefits of IEEE

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organisation, with students and researchers from all around the world, and our college is an official chapter of IEEE. This is a unique opportunity for all those who are looking for exposure in their fields backed by strong technical content provided by IEEE. IEEE offers an array of benefits.

Benefits

Keep Technically Current

Continue to advance your career and keep abreast of new and changing technologies. IEEE has the means for you to remain technically current through award winning publications, and technical societies.

Networking

Attending events of relevant interest, not only provides us technical knowledge but also the powerful possibility of networking with peer members. Student Congresses organized by IEEE are the ultimate events where you can network with a lot of volunteers and who created an impact at various scales

Technical Society

IEEE has around 39 technical societies which offers potential possibilities for the members. Joining a suitable society of your interest, can provide you more relevant technical updates related to your field.

Online Benefits

IEEE Collabratec is a social network which can be used to collaborate with your contacts. IEEE ResumeLab lets you create and customise your resume from various styles/templates.

Volunteering opportunities

IEEE also provides immense volunteering opportunities to exhibit our skills and to learn new things. It helps building a community of interested people and leaders who can innovate and do things. You can start volunteering from your Student Branch (SB), and can grow as a volunteer for Section, Council and Regional levels.


Other Benefits


  • You can access IEEE resources and their publications at any time, free of any cost.
  • IEEE is the forum where tech giants like Sony, Nokia, Tesla and Samsung publish their research papers and it isn't available anywhere else. You will have a first hand access to all of these.
  • The famous Bell Labs, publishes all its papers on IEEE forum. Just so you know, Bell Labs is credited with the invention of the Transistor, the C language, the DSP ECE students use in their Labs, LASER and the Solar cell.
  • You can have the latest News from the IEEE Spectrum, IEEE standars and The Institute.
The world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.
